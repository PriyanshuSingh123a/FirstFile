Overview

Welcome to christ and it's campus life. CHRIST (Deemed to be University) prides itself on its environmentally
friendly peaceful atmosphere and strong commitment to upholding a values-based system. The university
expects its students to maintain high standards in their attire, behavior, and discipline. It is important for students to show respect to their teachers by greeting them both on and off campus. Upon entering the classroom, students are expected to stand instructed to sit or until the teacher is seated. During attendance, each student should stand and respond when their name or register number is called. Punctuality is emphasized, requiring students to be seated promptly when the bell rings. Actions will be taken against students who disrupt corridors or create noise.
